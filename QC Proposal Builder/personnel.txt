PERSONNEL
Our field representatives are trained through various governmental and technical training programs.  Concrete testing training is obtained from completing a Grade 1 course provided by the American Concrete Institute (ACI), as well as certifications from the International Code Council (ICC).  Density testing training is obtained at density course provided by Ferris State University/Lawrence Tech in association with MDOT.  Structural steel and masonry certification is obtained through the ICC.  Radiation safety training for use and transport of nuclear moisture/density gauges is obtained from approved training entities.
